If you aren't looking for a full cake but still the pop of sugar and flavor a Whit's Kake, try one of our many kupcakes! Personal sized cakes in the classic flavors you love and unique flavors we created. Whatever you are looking for, we hope we can replicate for you! Have a sweet day!

Classic Flavors:

  • Vanilla with Vanilla or Chocolate frosting
  • Chocolate with Vanilla or Chocolate frosting
  • Marble with Vanilla icing
  • Strawberry with Vanilla or Strawberry frosting
  • Carrot with Cream Cheese frosting
  • Confetti! with rainbow colored buttercream
  • Red Velvet with Cream Cheese frosting
  • Coffee cupcake with a coffee infused butterceam

If you want to change it up a little bit, try one of our unique flavors:

  • Oreo cupcake with Vanilla frosting, topped with crushed Oreos
  • Peanut Butter Cup: Peanut butter cupcake with Chocolate frosting, topped with Reese's Pieces
  • Lemonade: Lemon cupcake with Vanilla and Lemon swirl frosting and topped with a candy lemon wedge
  • Mint Chip: Chocolate cupcake with Mint frosting and topped with chocolate chunks
  • Cookie Dough: Vanilla and Chocolate Chip cupcake with a Vanilla and cinnamon frosting topped with chocolate chip cookie dough
  • Cinnamon Roll: Cinnamon cupcake with Vanilla frosting and decorated with vanilla drizzle
  • Sundae: Chocolate cupcake with Vanilla filling, topped with a scoop of Chocolate buttercream, rainbow sprinkles, and a cherry on top!
  • White Chocolate and Raspberry: Vanilla cupcake with Raspberry filling and White Chocolate frosting topped with a Raspberry
